Our Solutions

1. NFC Payments

Fast, secure, and offline-ready transactions using smart NFC cards or bands ‚Äî no need for cash or QR scanning. Prepaid top-up cards/bands with custom branding and analytics.

2. Recharge & Issuance System

Issue NFC cards/bands to attendees with cash or UPI. Track and manage top-ups via a dedicated volunteer role. On-ground top-up counters for seamless attendee experience.

3. Access Control

Grant controlled access to zones like VIP areas, green rooms, or backend zones by scanning NFC cards at entry points. No more stamps or paper slips.

4. Event Ticketing

Generate, manage, and validate digital or physical tickets. Link ticket info to the NFC card/band for frictionless entry. Vendor settlement and real-time validation.

5. Volunteer Management

Create and manage event volunteer accounts with custom roles (e.g., Outlet Volunteer, Top-Up Volunteer). Track their activity and give limited access as per role. Everything is connected for smooth coordination.

6. Analytics & Reports

Get real-time insights on spending, top-ups, outlet sales, and volunteer activity. Generate post-event reports for review. Centralized dashboard for organizers and admins.

7. Custom Event Dashboard

Centralized dashboard for managing everything ‚Äî from attendees to money flow, tickets, and access. Custom pricing plans for every client, regardless of event size.

How It Works

  1. Organizer chooses a TapNex package (per card + setup)
  2. On-ground card/band top-up counters are set up
  3. Attendees pay a small activation fee
  4. Tap to pay at stalls
  5. Organizer monitors transactions live

Revenue Model

  • Package-based pricing for organizers (per card fee + setup fee)
  • Activation fee collected from customers
  • Leftover balance in card/band
  • Customized price for every client
