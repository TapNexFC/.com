Our Solutions

Payments

NFC Payments

Fast, secure, and offline-ready transactions using smart NFC cards or bands — no need for cash or QR scanning. Prepaid top-up cards/bands with custom branding and analytics.

Recharge & Issuance System

Issue NFC cards/bands to attendees with cash or UPI. Track and manage top-ups via a dedicated volunteer role. On-ground top-up counters for seamless attendee experience.

Access & Ticketing

Access Control

Grant controlled access to zones like VIP areas, green rooms, or backend zones by scanning NFC cards at entry points. No more stamps or paper slips.

Event Ticketing

Generate, manage, and validate digital or physical tickets. Link ticket info to the NFC card/band for frictionless entry. Vendor settlement and real-time validation.

Management & Analytics

Volunteer Management

Create and manage event volunteer accounts with custom roles (e.g., Outlet Volunteer, Top-Up Volunteer). Track their activity and give limited access as per role. Everything is connected for smooth coordination.

Analytics & Reports

Get real-time insights on spending, top-ups, outlet sales, and volunteer activity. Generate post-event reports for review. Centralized dashboard for organizers and admins.

Custom Event Dashboard

Centralized dashboard for managing everything — from attendees to money flow, tickets, and access. Custom pricing plans for every client, regardless of event size.

Frequently Asked Questions

NFC (Near Field Communication) is a short-range wireless technology that enables secure data transfer between two devices. In TapNex, we use NFC for contactless payments and access control, allowing users to simply tap their card or band to make transactions or gain entry to designated areas.
TapNex employs multiple layers of security including encrypted transactions, secure offline processing, and real-time monitoring. Each NFC card/band has unique identifiers and our system tracks all transactions, making it significantly more secure than traditional cash handling.
TapNex works with any NFC-enabled reader device. Our system is compatible with both Android and iOS devices for management purposes. For payments and access control, we provide dedicated NFC readers that work seamlessly with our cards and bands.
Yes! TapNex is designed to work seamlessly in offline environments. Our system can process transactions and store data locally, then sync once internet connectivity is restored. This makes it perfect for events in areas with limited connectivity.
We have a straightforward process for refunds and settlements. Organizers can process refunds through the admin dashboard, and vendor settlements are handled automatically based on transaction records. All transactions are logged and can be exported for accounting purposes.
